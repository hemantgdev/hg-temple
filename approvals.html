Approvals Needed

Based on the target markets I've mentioned, we have 2 choices in both USA & UAE, to either launch as a medical device or as a wellness device. It makes far greater sense to launch as a wellness device due to the longer timelines and uncertainty surround medical device clearance.

We would just need to be mindful of avoiding certain phrases that claim to diagnose, treat, screen for, or prevent diseases or medical conditions. However, even if we opt for the wellness route:

  1. We need to get a certain list of approvals that shouldn't take longer than 3 months.
  2. We should look to apply for FDA approval as soon as possible, for those features can be major differentiators when compared to the competition.
